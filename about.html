Jubair Sifat

Quran Reciter | Writer | Singer

Jubair Sifat, new star of Bangladeshi Modern Islamic music. He is an excellent lyricist, renowned music composer and International Quran Reciter. His melodious voice captivates everyone. His songs inspire and entertain all classes of people and send a message of peace and hope to the mass community. In "YouTube" Jubair Sifat is very famous for his Islamic meaningful lyrics. He is also renowned in social media platforms. But he is popular in Modern society as a successful lyricist. A number of nasheeds written and composed by him reached the peak of popularity. And this unique tune has reached the remote areas of Bangladesh. So far he has written more than 150+ Islamic songs and 500+ poems. Moreover, about a hundred songs have been released on various social media. He is currently associated with various cultural platforms including the biggest Islamic production houses in Bangladesh, such as Holy Tune, Haven Tune, Tarana, and other platforms

Born: January 1, 2004 (age 21)

Residence: Dhaka, Bangladesh

Nationality: Bangladeshi

Education: Islamic Studies

Religion: Islam

Parents: Sohel Rana, Hosneara Khatun
